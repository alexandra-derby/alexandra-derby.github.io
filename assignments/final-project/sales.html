Kona Hei Hei

Light, fast, and capable. You’ve got speed and agility, and the Hei Hei Supreme is right there with you. SRAM XX1 Eagle 12-speed drives carbon WTB Ci31 TCS rims with tubeless-ready Maxxis tires, a 120mm RockShox RS-1 fork and Monarch XX shock, SRAM Guide RSC brakes, and a RockShox Reverb with 1x lever. With nothing left to worry about, it’s just you and your favorite section of singletrack. $8999 USD.

Kona Lava Dome

Making bikes that are dependable, capable and built for everyday riding is what we are known for. Based on a renowned XC frame that is light, durable and fun, the Lava Dome, with its big 29-inch wheels, is just as capable on trails as it is bombing around banged-up streets. Consider this your SUV of the bicycle world. $500 USD.

Kona Precept 150

The Precept 150 has the body of an efficient trail bike and an attitude that is begging for downhill runs. With capable 27.5-inch wheels, wildly efficient Swinger Independent Suspension and 150mm of blazing-fast downhill travel, the Precept 150 delivers so much performance you’ll feel like you’re cheating the next time you ride. $2000 USD.
