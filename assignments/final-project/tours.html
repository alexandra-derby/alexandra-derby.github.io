Book your adventure tours with us for an unbeatable experience!

We provide cycling adventures for small groups that include vehicle support, excellent food, and charming inns. On guided cycling trips, the knowledge of your guides helps you enjoy the route and its options in an informed way, at your own pace. We propose scheduled dates, but are able to add new departures to enjoy privately or invite others to join.

Unguided Half Day Bike Tours:

This ride is aimed at beginner to intermediate riders. Refreshing and beautiful scenery have earned this tour 5 stars. Allow 4 hours for this adventure. Plan on biking for about 2 hours. Southeast Bozeman near the waterfront. Rates start at $20 per person.

Guided Full Day Bike Tours:

This ride is geared toward intermediate to advanced intermediate riders. Give yourself the greater part of the day for this ride. Riding time is usually between 3-4 hours. South Bozeman around the lake. Rates start at $50 per person.

Guided Week Long Bike Tours:

Starting August and through September, join us for a week of mountain biking! Please call us at (406) 409-2273 for more information.

On week-long trips we move your luggage for you, transfer you along the route when planned, and are just a phone call away for suggestions or if you need help. Self-guided tours are available for as few as two participants, on any dates you choose.
